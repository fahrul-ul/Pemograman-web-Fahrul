Spacious

Guests can enjoy a relaxed yet upscale experience, whether savoring gourmet meals, engaging in lively conversations, or hosting special gatherings. The spacious layout ensures privacy and ease of movement, making every dining moment effortless and enjoyable.

Chef de Cuisine Nathan Rueff

Chef Nathan's culinary journey began at 14, inspired by his grandparents' Parisian restaurant. He refined his skills in prestigious kitchens such as the Royal Monceau with Chef Roberto Rispoli, contributing to the achievement of two Michelin stars at 'La Scene.' In 2020, he joined Jumeirah Mina Al Salam and Jumeirah Al Qasr. At Commodore Club, his dishes blend comfort and elegance, showcasing his culinary finesse.
